Site Plan for GRAMDO/RS Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The chosen site name is GRAMDO/RS Chamber of Commerce, reflecting the region and its focus on regional business growth and support.

Site Purpose

The GRAMDO/RS Chamber of Commerce endeavors to:

  • Promote regional economic growth and business development
  • Foster community engagement and collaboration

Scenarios

  • What events are scheduled this month to facilitate business networking?
  • Where can I find the contact details of the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the recent population growth in the GRAMDO/RS region?

Color Schema

The primary color for headings is #008080, and the background color is #f5f5f5.

These colors will be used consistently throughout the site for branding and readability.

Typography

The site will predominantly use the 'Arial' font family for content and headings.
